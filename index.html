All releases & notes

100% local

Transcription runs on your Mac with whisper.cpp and Metal acceleration. No cloud, no account, no subscription — ever.

Meeting-aware

Detects when a Zoom meeting starts or ends and offers to start or stop recording, so you never forget to capture one.

Transparent by design

Optional spoken announcements tell participants when recording starts and stops, and the menu-bar icon pulses red while capturing.

Minutes with Claude

Optionally turn any transcript into actionable meeting minutes with the bundled Claude Code skill — one click, fully on your terms.

Getting started

  1. Download the DMG and drag Scribe into Applications.
  2. Launch it — Scribe lives in your menu bar (look for the waveform icon near the clock).
  3. Follow the welcome guide to grant Screen & System Audio Recording and Microphone permissions.
  4. Click Start Recording in a meeting. Transcripts land in your chosen folder as plain text.
Scribe updates itself automatically via a signed update feed.
Everything is processed on-device. Recording conversations may require participant consent where you live — announce it.
